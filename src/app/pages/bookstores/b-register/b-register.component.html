Guidelines to register your Book Shop

  • We require you to fill the following forms and send them to us using registered post.
  • After we receive your documents, we will verify your bookshop(s) and regiter you to our system. Login credentials willbe emailed to the email address provided.
  • If you own a bookshop chain, we consider each branch as a seperate bookshop. Therefore, you will have to provide details of the branches you wish to register to the system (not necessarily all the branches).
  • You will be provided login details for each branch that can be managed by the branch manager, and one master account to handle all branches.
  • In the meantime, you may email the list of A/L and O/L study materials available at your bookshop. This needs to be provided seperately for each branch.
  • The registration fees are:
    Rs. 300 - single-branch bookshop
    Rs. 500 - bookshop chain with 2 to 5 branches
    Rs. 800 - bookshop chain with 6 or more branches
    that is registered to our system.
  • The registration fee will be considered as the subscription fee for the 1st month of your registration. You can add as many O/L and A/L study materials as you want during this time.
  • From the 2nd month of your registration, a subscription fee of Rs 50.00 will be charged for each new book that is uploaded to our system. The fee will be charged at the end of the month.
  • You are requested to pay the registration fee and enclose the bank slip or email the online banking receipt while you are sending in your documents.
Please download the required form set for your scenario, fill them and send them to us over the registered post to Get Started! An Instruction guide is attached with both the document sets listed below.
